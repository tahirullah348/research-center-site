Bio

Dr. Tahir Ullah Khan holds a Ph.D. in Mathematics and has been serving as an Assistant Professor of Mathematics in the Higher Education Department, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan, since 2024. He previously served as a Lecturer in the same department from 2018 to 2024 and also taught at the Shaikh Zayed Islamic Centre, University of Peshawar. He is the Founder and Director of the Research Center for Mathematical and Applied Sciences, Bannu, Pakistan. His research interests include fractional calculus, neural network operators, mathematical inequalities, and fractional differential equations, with a strong emphasis on real-world applications. Dr. Khan has published extensively in leading international journals.
