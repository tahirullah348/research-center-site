About Us

The Research Center for Mathematical and Applied Sciences (RCMAS) is dedicated to pioneering research at the intersection of mathematics and real-world applications. We focus on fractional calculus, stochastic analysis, neural network operators, and inequality theory, contributing to advanced modeling in physics, finance, engineering, and computational sciences.

Research Areas

  • Fractional Differential Equations and Operators
  • Stochastic Processes and Neural Network Models
  • Hermite-Hadamard and Jensen-type Inequalities
  • Applications in Image Restoration, NIDS, and Control Theory
